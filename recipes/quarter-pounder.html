Enjoy The Quarter Pounder

The Quarter Pounder is a popular hamburger from McDonald's, known for its hefty 1/4-pound beef patty. It’s typically topped with onions, pickles, ketchup, mustard, and cheese, all served on a sesame seed bun. Its savory flavor and satisfying size make it a go-to for those craving a classic, filling fast-food burger.

The Quarter Pounder is a classic fast-food burger featuring a juicy, quarter-pound beef patty. It’s usually paired with melted cheese, fresh onions, tangy pickles, and a squirt of ketchup and mustard, all nestled in a soft sesame seed bun. Its bigger patty offers a heartier bite compared to regular burgers, making it a favorite for those looking for a more substantial meal.
