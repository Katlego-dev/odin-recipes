The All New Double Cheese Burger

The McDonald's Double Cheeseburger features two savory all-beef patties, two slices of melty American cheese, pickles, onions, ketchup, and mustard, all served on a classic hamburger bun. It's a simple, hearty option with a rich, cheesy flavor.

The Double Cheeseburger is a satisfying, no-frills burger with two juicy beef patties and melted cheese. Topped with tangy pickles, onions, and classic condiments, it delivers a balanced, flavorful bite in every layer.

Ingredients

  • Two all-beef patties
  • Two slices of American cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onions
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Regular hamburger bun

Steps

  1. Toast the buns – Lightly toast the top and bottom hamburger buns.
  2. Cook the beef patties – Grill two all-beef patties until fully cooked.
  3. Add cheese – Place a slice of American cheese on each beef patty as it finishes cooking, allowing it to melt.
  4. Prepare the bottom bun – Spread ketchup and mustard on the bottom bun.
  5. Add pickles and onions – Place pickles and onions on top of the condiments.
  6. Place the first beef patty – Add the first beef patty (with melted cheese) on top of the bottom bun.
  7. Place the second beef patty – Add the second beef patty (with melted cheese) on top of the first patty.
  8. Assemble the burger – Top the stacked patties with the top bun to complete the Double Cheeseburger.
